Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Arena Loop?

Each level shows you a room full of pixel art objects. Memorize it carefully. After a brief flash, one thing changes — an object might be added, removed, moved, recolored, or flipped. Tap on the change to advance to the next level.

What happens if I tap the wrong thing?

You receive a strike. Three strikes and it's game over. The timer also resets after a strike, so use that second chance wisely.

What happens if time runs out?

Running out of time also costs you a strike. Keep an eye on the timer bar at the top of the screen.

How does the difficulty work?

Levels get progressively harder. You'll see more objects to track, subtler anomalies (single pixel changes, wall shifts), shorter memorization time, and a tighter countdown timer as you advance.

How do I restore my "Remove Ads" purchase?

Go to Settings within the game and tap "Restore Purchases." Your purchase will be restored automatically if you previously bought it with the same Apple ID.

How do I see my score on the leaderboard?

Make sure you're signed into Game Center on your device (Settings → Game Center). Your high score is submitted automatically after each game. Tap "Leaderboard" on the title screen to view rankings.

Any tips for spotting anomalies?

Scan the room methodically — don't rush. Focus on colors and positions. Edge objects are easy to miss. Try to build a mental snapshot of the layout before the flash.

How do I remove ads?

You can purchase "Remove Ads" from the Settings screen or the Game Over screen. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all banner and interstitial ads.
